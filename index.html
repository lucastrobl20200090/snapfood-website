SnapFood Project Overview

SnapFood is a calorie tracking app that allows users to monitor their daily calorie intake. Unlike most other programs, SnapFood offers a unique feature: instead of manually entering food items, users can take photos of their meals. The app will then automatically estimate the calorie content of the food shown in the pictures.

Key Features

  • Calorie Tracking: Traditional feature where users input their meals and drinks and track calorie consumption.
  • Automatic Calorie Estimation: Using AI-powered image recognition, the app estimates calories based on photos uploaded by the user.
  • User-friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for easy food entry and photo uploading.
  • History and Analysis: Users can track calorie consumption over time to understand and optimize their eating habits.
